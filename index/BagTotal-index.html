How they impact the environment

Bags have a detrimental effect on the environment by causing pollution through littering, entanglement, and ingestion by wildlife, decomposing slowly, releasing hazardous chemicals during the process, and requiring a large amount of fossil fuels to produce, which leads to the contamination of soil and waterways with microplastics.
